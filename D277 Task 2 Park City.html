Home Salt Lake City St. George Park City

Park City is were the Winter 2002 Olymics were held. It is a popular vacation spot nestled in the mountains. Here are some interesting facts about Park City:

  1. Population(as of 2020): 8,400
  2. Established: 1869
  3. Region: Northeastern indent corner
  4. Classification: Sub-Urban
  5. Median Household Income: $88,438 (24th of 315 cities)
