Salt Lake is the capital of Utah and is also the biggest city. Here are some interesting facts about Salt Lake:

  1. Population(as of 2020): 200,000
  2. Established: 1847
  3. Region: Just Southeast of the Great Salt Lake
  4. Classification: Urban
  5. Median Household Income: $45,833 (243rd of 315 cities)
